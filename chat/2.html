There are a few ways to create a mobile app that allows users to send files from one device to another without an internet connection.
One approach is to use a technology called "peer-to-peer" (P2P) networking. P2P allows devices to connect directly to one another and share files without the need for a central server.
Another approach is to use a technology called "Wi-Fi Direct." Wi-Fi Direct allows devices to connect to each other directly over Wi-Fi, without the need for a wireless access point. This can be used to send files from one device to another without an internet connection.
It is also important to consider the security and privacy of the files being transferred, and to implement appropriate measures such as encryption.
